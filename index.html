BayesAI is a workshop on probabilistic and Bayesian aspects of artificial intelligence to be held at Lancaster University from midday Monday, 23 September to midday Thursday, 26 September.

The workshop seeks to catalyse research at the interface of Bayesian statistics and artificial intelligence with themes including:
  • Generative modeling.
  • Physics-informed neural networks.
  • Monte Carlo methods in machine learning.
Call for submissions: we invite participants to submit posters for the evening session on Monday 23 September.

Schedule

Planned events include:
  • Invited talks in morning and afternoon sessions.
  • A poster session on Monday, 23 September.
  • A workshop dinner on Wednesday, 25 September.
A detailed programme will be released closer to the date.
